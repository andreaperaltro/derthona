# Derthona Basket

This file is the machine-readable companion to the
[Derthona Basket Brand Manual](index.html). The website is the complete visual
reference; this document provides concise brand context for AI tools and
text-based workflows.

## Strategy

### Overview

Derthona Basket is a contemporary basketball club shaped by heritage, culture
and sporting ambition. Its identity transforms the history of Tortona into a
living system built for the present and the future.

The lion recalls where the club comes from. The rose expresses what it carries
forward: courage, generosity and grace. The ambition is to strengthen the
connection between club, city and community while building Derthona's future
on the European stage.

### Positioning

Derthona Basket is a contemporary basketball institution rooted in the
heritage of Tortona.

It is:

- historically grounded, never nostalgic;
- institutional, consistent and built for longevity;
- basketball-first and connected to the language of the modern game;
- distinctive without being decorative;
- ambitious without losing authenticity.

It is not a trend-led sports identity, a generic entertainment brand or a
heritage system frozen in the past.

### Personality

The brand is confident, authentic, clear, respectful and ambitious.

It should feel:

- distinctive;
- timeless and contemporary;
- rooted and evolving;
- European in ambition;
- close to its community;
- unmistakably basketball.

It should never feel arrogant, artificial, ornamental, generic or driven by
short-lived trends.

### Promise

Derthona Basket turns history into forward momentum.

The brand commits to:

- representing Tortona with clarity and pride;
- bringing club, city and community together;
- pursuing excellence on and off the court;
- evolving the identity without losing its roots.

Primary brand statement: **Rooted in history. Built for tomorrow.**

### Guardrails

- History is a foundation to build upon, not a decorative reference.
- Every visual or verbal choice must feel intentional.
- Basketball remains central to the identity.
- Authority must never become arrogance.
- Ambition must remain authentic and connected to the community.
- Tradition may evolve; the identity must endure.

Litmus test: if the work could belong to any other basketball club, it is not
distinctive enough for Derthona.

### Tonal Rules

- Be confident, never arrogant.
- Be authentic: express identity rather than following trends.
- Be clear, direct and intentional.
- Be respectful and inclusive by nature.
- Be ambitious: think forward and aim higher.
- Prefer concise, declarative sentences.
- Connect history to action and future momentum.
- Use basketball language naturally, not as decoration.
- Avoid generic sports hype and inflated marketing language.
- Preserve the dignity of an institution while retaining human energy.

| We Say | We Never Say |
|---|---|

## Visual

### Colors

The palette contains three equal primary colors:

- **Tortona Red** — `#EF3340`, Pantone 032 C, RGB 239 / 51 / 64.
  Use for the rose, accents, calls to action and high-impact moments.
- **Derthona Black** — `#2D2926`, Pantone Black C, RGB 45 / 41 / 38.
  Use for primary typography, marks and dark surfaces.
- **Derthona White** — `#FFFFFF`, Pantone White 000 C, RGB 255 / 255 / 255.
  Use for light surfaces, reversed artwork and contrast.

Red brings the heritage of Tortona into the system. Black and white carry the
club's historic identity. Do not introduce additional brand colors without
approval.

### Typography

#### Derthona Display

Custom display typeface, Regular. Use for headlines, campaign statements,
scores and high-impact messages. This is the identity typeface.

The font includes OpenType Stylistic Set 1 (`ss01`) with alternate uppercase
forms for A, B, D, E, F, H, K, P, Q, R and T. The feature is present in OTF,
TTF, WOFF and WOFF2.

Approved files:

- [OTF](assets/fonts/DerthonaDisplay-Regular.otf)
- [TTF](assets/fonts/DerthonaDisplay-Regular.ttf)
- [WOFF](assets/fonts/DerthonaDisplay-Regular.woff)
- [WOFF2](assets/fonts/DerthonaDisplay-Regular.woff2)

#### PP Neue Montreal

Use for subtitles, body copy, navigation, captions and functional information.
This is the information typeface.

#### PP Fragment

Use for editorial accents, quotes, manifesto excerpts and expressive moments.
This is the editorial typeface.

### Clear Space

X is defined by the lion symbol. Internal proportions and a minimum clear space
of 3X must always be maintained. Use the
[official respect-area construction](assets/official/respect-area.svg).

### Composed Logos

Composed logos balance Derthona Basket with an approved partner identity.

- Always apply the larger respect area required by either logo.
- Separate the marks with a 1 pt line as wide as the larger logo.
- Use a black or white separator according to the background.
- Use the supplied horizontal or vertical composition.
- Select the supplied color or monochrome version for the intended light or
  dark background.

### Style

The visual language is built around **The Curve**: a graphic gesture where
basketball and territory meet.

The Curve echoes the trajectory of the ball, the energy of the Curva, the
hills surrounding Tortona and the vaulted forms of Pier Luigi Nervi’s
Magazzini del Sale.

Through repetition and scale, these references become a dynamic and
distinctive graphic language. Use centered, offset or architectural variations
from the approved masters in
[the visual-language library](index.html#visuals).

Design keywords: rooted, dynamic, architectural, intentional, contemporary,
distinctive, basketball-first.
